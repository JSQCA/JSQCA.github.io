Protecting our residents from unfair practices is a primary concern of the JSQCA. Below are resources to help tenants understand and exercise their rights.

Jersey City Resources

  • Jersey City Tenant Resources – Official city portal for tenant protections and assistance.

Jersey City Together

Jersey City Together has been a strong partner in advocating for renter protections. They offer several resources:

  • Rent Control Fact Sheet
  • Illegal Rent Complaint Form
  • Documentation Guide for tenants
  • Landlord Accountability resources

Need Help?

If you are experiencing issues with your landlord or have questions about your rights as a tenant, attend one of our monthly meetings. We can connect you with legal resources and advocacy organizations that can help.

Meetings are held on the second Monday of every month at 7 PM at 71 Sip Ave., 6th Floor, Jersey City.
