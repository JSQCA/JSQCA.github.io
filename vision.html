Working together to build a better community — Clean, Green, and Safe.

The Journal Square Community Association represents residents, owners of residential property, and businesses in the Journal Square area.

We serve as a voice for the community in dealing with the city, county, developers, local businesses, and other community groups. We bring people together at monthly meetings so they can connect with their neighbors and remain updated about our neighborhood.

Membership is open to all residents of Jersey City, owners of residential property, and businesses within the boundaries set forth in our by-laws. The JSQCA is recognized by the IRS as a 501(c)(3) organization, so member dues and donations are tax-deductible.

Monthly Meetings

We normally meet from 7 PM to 9 PM on the second Monday of the month at 71 Sip Ave., 6th Floor. Our meetings are also streamed via Facebook.

In addition to monthly meetings, members can join committees that address some of the concerns of the Association. Learn more about our committees.
