Committees

Art

Promotes arts and culture in Journal Square through public art projects, murals, and creative community events.

Chair: George Zalepa

Clean

Ensures that sidewalks, streets, and properties are clean, litter-free, and properly maintained through education and enforcement.

Chair: Travis Miscia

Communications & Outreach

Coordinates communications with the community and members. Seeks to expand the membership base with an emphasis on increasing diversity.

Chairs: Margaret Doman

Construction & Planning

Works closely with city officials and developers to ensure new construction projects provide maximum public benefit.

Chair: Chris Lamm

Green

Focuses on parks including the Bergen Arches, Courthouse Park, and Pavonia/Marion Park, as well as environmental sustainability.

Chair: Megan Santosusso

History

Engages, educates, and inspires residents about Journal Square's rich history through speaking series and walking tours. Organizes annual Bergen Square Day.

Chair: James Dievler

Safety

Connects community members with law enforcement and spearheads initiatives to enhance neighborhood safety and improve street security.

Chairs: Renee Tirella Cassidy

Transportation

Advocates for pedestrians, cyclists, transit users, and drivers. Focuses on traffic safety, walkability, and public transit access.

Chair: Adam Cohen
