Meetings

Meetings are open to the public and are a great opportunity to meet your neighbors, find out what is happening in the community, and share your voice. Guest speakers have included Mayor Steven Fulop, State Senator Brian Stack, and other civic leaders.

Held the second Monday of every month at 7 PM at 71 Sip Ave., 6th Floor.

Individual Membership

Membership is only $25 per person annually. It affords you a vote in board elections and formal organizational decisions. The JSQCA is a registered 501(c)(3), so membership cost is tax deductible.

Business Membership

Businesses in the Journal Square area can join for $100 per year. Enjoy networking opportunities, increased promotion, active participation in community events, and access to essential resources.
