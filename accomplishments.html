The Journal Square Community Association has worked to improve our neighborhood through advocacy, partnerships, and direct action.

  • Housing Policy Collaborated with City Council and Planning officials to create an affordable housing amendment specific to Journal Square.
  • Loews Theatre Led a successful petition campaign that accelerated repairs to the theatre annex and expedited removal of scaffolding on Gloria Esposito Way.
  • Public Art Commissioned multiple murals throughout the neighborhood, bringing color and culture to Journal Square's streetscape.
  • Street Infrastructure Initiated a complete street petition focused on Bergen Avenue to improve safety and walkability.
  • Community Partnerships Established alliances with the Journal Square Special Improvement District, Old Bergen Church, Jersey City Together, and Friends of the Loews.
  • Parks Development Secured funding and advanced planning for upgrades to Brett Triangle Park.
  • Historical Programming Organized and sponsored walking tours highlighting the Journal Square area's rich history.
  • Civic Engagement Co-sponsored mayoral and Ward C City Council debates, giving residents direct access to candidates.
